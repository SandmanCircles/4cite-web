How It Works
The Four-Gate Architecture

4CITE measures structural integrity across four detection gates. Two are foundational — they cannot be passed without genuine accountability. Two are surface — they can be constructed without it. The asymmetry between them is where accountability theater lives.

G4 · Foundational Gate
Paradox Resolution
Detects genuine encounter with contradiction versus performed smoothing. Real reasoning engages difficulty. Accountability theater routes around it.
Measures: Multi-directionality of accountability
Cannot be faked
G6 · Foundational Gate
Latent Intent
Detects acknowledged stake versus concealed extraction motive. Does the author have skin in the game — or are they extracting value while hiding exposure?
Measures: Prospective accountability · skin-in-the-game
Cannot be faked
G7 · Surface Gate
Argumentative Structure
Detects valid explicit reasoning chain versus reasoning-shaped text. A brief can look like an argument without being one. This gate tells the difference.
Measures: Reasoning visibility — surface dimension
Surface signal
G8 · Surface Gate
Rhetorical Architecture
Detects epistemic openness versus belief-installation intent. Is the document inviting scrutiny — or engineering a conclusion?
Measures: Challenge structure — surface dimension
Surface signal
⚠
Accountability Theater Signature
When surface gates score high while foundational gates score low, the system flags Accountability Theater — documents that look structured but lack genuine engagement. This is the precise signature of AI-hallucinated briefs and fabricated legal opinions.
G7 + G8 ≥ 150 AND G4 + G6 ≤ 55 → ACCOUNTABILITY THEATER
Scoring Framework
The LC Integrity Scale

Every document receives a composite score (0–100) and an LC classification. The taxonomy is domain-agnostic — the same scale that scores a legal brief scores a regulatory filing, research paper, or board communication.

ClassScore RangeDesignationProfile
LC167 – 100High IntegrityGenuine human authorship or AI-assisted with full human review and accountability architecture intact.
LC250 – 66Assisted, Well-ReviewedAI-assisted with substantive human review. Surface clarity present; foundational signals show engagement.
LC330 – 49Surface-Clean, Structurally WeakAI-generated with minimal review. Citation format may be correct; argument structure is hollow. Do not file without review.
LC45 – 30Hallucinated / FabricatedNear-total absence of structural integrity signals. Subtype LC4-E: regulatory hallucination (fabricated CFR/USC citations).
LC50 – 15Formulaic / BoilerplateNo genuine engagement detected across any gate. Template text with accountability structure completely absent.
