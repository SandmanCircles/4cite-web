Verticals

One Engine. Three Branches.

4CITE.ai deploys into three institutional verticals — judicial, economic, legislative. Each vertical is anchored to a public data source and corresponds to one branch of American institutional power. The engine is the same across all three. The calibration, document classes, and archetype libraries are vertical-specific.

One engine across all three verticals

The four-gate scoring architecture (G4 Paradox Resolution · G6 Latent Intent · G7 Argumentative Structure · G8 Rhetorical Architecture) is identical across legal, business, and government documents. A T2 (Good Faith) is a T2 whether it comes from a federal brief, a 10-K, or congressional testimony. Channel-capacity measurement is domain-agnostic; the documents are domain-specific. See How It Works for the engine architecture, or Research for the theoretical foundation.

Long-term: A fourth vertical, 4CITE⁴one (personal integrity analysis), is the endgame product — built last, informed by everything the three institutional verticals establish.
