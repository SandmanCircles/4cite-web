Unit Economics

What Is a Unit

Units are consumed each time you run a pipeline action on a document. The same unit currency works across all three verticals — there's no law-only budget or gov-only budget. Run your units where your work is.

Unit consumption per action

ScanRuns the three-act integrity scan. Produces observations, secondary questions, and the Big Question.
ReportRuns the four independent gates (G4/G6/G7/G8) and Synthesis pass. Requires a preceding Scan.
CertifyPackages Scan + Report output into an immutable certificate record and stores in Cert Vault.
Corpus queryQuery the scored corpus for longitudinal analysis, entity scoring, or comparable documents.

Exact unit costs per action are set at beta launch and displayed in your account usage dashboard.

Refunds & Cancellations

Refunds & Cancellations

Plain-language summary of how refunds, cancellations, and renewals work at 4CITE.ai. These terms are part of your purchase. The full legal version lives in the Terms of Service.

Walk-in 4CITE Cert ($15) — pay-per

A 4CITE Cert is a finished digital deliverable. Once your certificate has been issued, recorded in the Public Registry, and delivered (PDF emailed and stored in your Cert Vault), the purchase is non-refundable. The certificate is permanent — there is no operational way to "un-issue" it. We will refund a Cert purchase only when a technical failure on our side prevents delivery (for example, the pipeline errors out and no certificate is produced). In that case, contact hello@4cite.ai within 14 days and we will refund in full or, at your option, re-run the analysis.

Subscriptions — 4CITE⁴one / 4CITE⁴more / 4CITE⁴all

Subscriptions are billed monthly in advance. You can cancel at any time from your account billing page, which opens the Stripe Customer Portal. Cancellation stops the next renewal — you keep full access and your remaining unit allotment until the end of the current billing period. We do not pro-rate partial months: no refund is issued for the unused portion of a period that has already started. Switching tiers mid-period is pro-rated by Stripe in the standard way (you are charged or credited the difference on your next invoice).

Top-up packs (100 / 500 / 2,000 units)

Top-up packs are non-refundable once purchased. Top-up units do not expire — they carry forward across billing periods until used, even if you downgrade or cancel your subscription, as long as your account remains open.

Enterprise

Enterprise terms are governed by the order form or master services agreement signed with 4 SHIELD LLC. Refund and cancellation rights for enterprise customers are set in that agreement and supersede the terms above.

Failed payments & chargebacks

If a renewal payment fails, Stripe will retry the charge per its standard dunning schedule and notify you by email. If the payment ultimately cannot be collected, the subscription will be paused — your account remains accessible but pipeline calls will return a 402 status until billing is resolved. Initiating a chargeback for a transaction where the service was successfully delivered may result in account suspension while we review the dispute.

To request a refund covered by the conditions above, or to ask any billing question, email hello@4cite.ai from the address on your account. We respond to billing inquiries within two business days.

Pricing Questions

Common Questions

What is the difference between the walk-in Cert and a subscription?

The walk-in 4CITE Cert ($15) requires no account — you paste a document, pay, and receive a certificate by email. Subscriptions give you a monthly unit allotment for ongoing analysis across all three verticals. If you need one certificate, use the walk-in. If you're running 15 or more analyses a month, a subscription is more economical.

What does "units work across all three verticals" mean?

The same unit allotment covers law, business, and government documents. A solo attorney on 4CITE⁴one can use their 225 monthly units on briefs, EDGAR filings, and regulatory documents in any combination — there is no per-vertical budget restriction.

Do unused units roll over?

Monthly subscription units reset at each billing period and do not roll over. Top-up pack units do not expire — they carry forward across billing periods until used. If you anticipate a high-volume month, purchasing a top-up pack in advance is more economical than relying on rollover.

Does a certificate expire?

No. Certificates stored in the Cert Vault are permanent, with no auto-expiration. The scoring is frozen at the moment of issuance. The user controls deletion — the system never deletes user data unilaterally.

Is the pricing shown here final?

Pricing shown is BETA pricing, locked as of April 2026. Post-beta pricing may differ. Beta participants will be notified before any pricing changes and offered the opportunity to lock beta rates.

Can I score documents privately?

Yes. Non-certified documents sit in your private account queue until you delete them. Research Corpus inclusion is opt-in only and defaults to off — you must actively choose to contribute. Certificate documents are stored permanently in the Cert Vault, but Vault storage is user-controlled for deletion.
